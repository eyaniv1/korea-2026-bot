# Korea 2026 Family Trip Itinerary
## April 23 – May 4, 2026 | Eran, Michal, Jonathan & Ofir

### Route: Seoul (3 nights) → Jeju (3 nights) → Busan (2 nights) → Seoul (3 nights)

---

## Day 1 — Apr 23 (Thu) | Arrive Seoul

- **10:50** Land at Incheon Airport. Pick up eSIMs, T-money cards
- AREX or taxi to hotel (~1 hr)
- **Late PM:** Walk around **Myeongdong** — people-watching, street food
- **Dinner:** **Yukjeon Hoekwan** — Michelin-listed bulgogi since 1966
- **Evening:** Stroll **Cheonggyecheon Stream** (lit up at night)

**Hotel:** Amid Hotel Seoul · Jongno/Insadong
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Myeongdong and Cheonggyecheon work fine in light rain. If heavy, underground shopping at Myeongdong Station or relax at hotel.

---

## Day 2 — Apr 24 (Fri) | Seoul Culture

- **Morning:** **Naksan Park** — city views
- **AM:** **Ihwa Mural Village** — street art
- **Lunch:** **Ssamjigil** — Insadong crafts + street food lunch
- **PM:** 🐱 **Themed Cafe** — Poop Cafe or animal cafe (Insadong)
- **PM:** **Jogyesa Temple** — lotus lanterns
- **PM:** **Seochon** — cafes, galleries
- **PM:** 🏛️ **Deoksugung Palace** — stone wall walk (on the way south)
- **Evening:** 💆 **Abijou Clinic** — Korean Aqua Peel facial
- **Dinner:** **Maple Tree House** (Samcheong-dong) — Korean BBQ
- **Night option:** ✨ **DDP at night** — Zaha Hadid landmark + LED rose garden

**Hotel:** Amid Hotel Seoul · Jongno/Insadong
**🌧️ Rain plan:** National Museum of Korea (free, world-class), COEX Mall underground, or Lotte World indoor theme park.

---

## Day 3 — Apr 25 (Sat) | Suwon + Tower + Jungsik

- **8:30 AM:** 🏯 Train to **Suwon Hwaseong Fortress** wall walk (5.7 km, UNESCO)
- **10:30 AM:** **Haenggung Palace** — martial arts + guard performances (weekends)
- **11:00 AM:** **Haenggung Street** — busking, crafts, galleries, street food
- **12:00 PM:** 🍗 **Suwon Chicken Street** — famous Korean fried chicken
- **2:00 PM:** **Namdaemun Market** — walk at your own pace
- **5:30 PM:** **N Seoul Tower** — cable car up, sunset at 7:10 PM
- **7:15 PM:** 📚 **Starfield Library COEX** — iconic bookshelves (optional)
- **8:00 PM:** **Jungsik** — Michelin-starred modern Korean (splurge night)

**Hotel:** Amid Hotel Seoul · Jongno/Insadong
**Transport:** 🚄 Seoul Station → Suwon (30 min train). Return same way after lunch.
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Skip Suwon fortress wall. Instead: War Memorial of Korea (free, indoor), Namdaemun Market (covered), then N Seoul Tower + Jungsik as planned.

---

## Day 4 — Apr 26 (Sun) | Fly to Jeju

- **Morning:** Jeju Airport — pick up Alamo rental car
- **Optional:** 😏 Jeju Loveland — quirky sculpture park (~30 min)
- **PM — Pick one hike:**
  - 🥾 **Option A: Saryeoni Forest Trail** (flat, relaxing) | 10 km · 2–3 hrs · +222m
  - 🥾 **Option B: Hallasan — Eoseungsaengak Trail** | 2.1 km · 1–1.5 hrs · +190m
- **Optional afternoon activities (especially with Option B):**
  - 🌋 **Manjanggul Lava Tube** — UNESCO volcanic cave (~40 min)
  - 🐱 **Jeju Maze Park** — hedge maze, mini-golf, cats (~1 hr)
  - 🏘️ **Jeju Folk Village Museum** — 100+ traditional buildings (~1.5 hrs)
  - 🌺 **Hallim Park** — 9 themed gardens + lava caves (~1.5 hrs)
- **Dinner:** **Dombedon** — Michelin-rated Jeju black pork BBQ
- **Evening:** 🥘 **Jeju Dongmun Market** — evening food market (near hotel)

**Hotel:** Grabel Hotel Jeju · Jeju City
**Transport:** ✈ Gimpo → Jeju (~1 hr) + 🚗 Alamo rental car
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Saryeoni is beautiful in light rain (misty). If heavy: Jeju Folk Village Museum or Nexon Computer Museum.

---

## Day 5 — Apr 27 (Mon) | Jeju E-Bike + Peak

- **8:00 AM:** **Seongsan Port** — early ferry to Udo Island (bring passport!)
- **8:30 AM:** 🚴 **E-bike Udo Island** — rent at Cheonjin Port | 15 km · 1.5–2 hrs · ~100m
- **~12:00 PM:** 🥾 **Seongsan Ilchulbong** (Sunrise Peak, after ferry back) | 1.8 km · 40–60 min · +142m

**Hotel:** Grabel Hotel Jeju
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Skip Udo (ferry may cancel). Instead: Manjanggul Lava Tube (underground, rain-proof) + Jeju Stone Park. Ilchulbong is fine in light rain.

---

## Day 6 — Apr 28 (Tue) | Jeju Coastal Day

- **Morning:** 🥾 **Olle Trail Section 7** (start at Oedolgae) | 17.6 km full / 8–10 km partial · +288m
- **Midday:** 🌊 **Jeongbang Waterfall** — falls into the sea (20 min stop)
- **PM:** **Jusangjeolli Cliff** — basalt columns
- **PM:** **O'sulloc Tea Museum** + green tea fields
- **PM:** **Hyeopjae Beach**

**Hotel:** Grabel Hotel Jeju
**Transport:** 🚗 Alamo rental car — last day driving. Return at Jeju Airport tomorrow morning.
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Skip Olle Trail. Keep Jusangjeolli (quick with umbrella), O'sulloc (indoor), add Spirited Garden or Jeju Glass Castle.

---

## Day 7 — Apr 29 (Wed) | Fly to Busan

- **Morning:** Return car at Jeju Airport + fly to Busan (~50 min)
- **PM:** **Gamcheon Culture Village** — colorful hillside art village
- **PM:** **Haeundae Beach** walk
- **Dinner:** **Amsogalbi-jip** — premium beef galbi
- **Evening:** 🏮 **Samgwangsa Temple** — 40,000 lotus lanterns (Buddha's Birthday season)

**Hotel:** L7 Haeundae by Lotte · Haeundae, Busan
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Gamcheon has covered alleys — works in rain. If heavy: Busan Museum of Art or Shinsegae Centum City.

---

## Day 8 — Apr 30 (Thu) | Busan + Gyeongju

- **9:15 AM:** **Yonggungsa Temple** — seaside (45 min)
- **11:30 AM:** **Bulguksa Temple** (UNESCO) — Gyeongju
- **1:30 PM:** 🥾 **Seokguram Grotto** — forest walk | 3 km · 1–1.5 hrs · +382m
- **5:30 PM:** **Hwangridan-gil** — dinner
- **6:30 PM:** 🪦 **Daereungwon Tomb Complex** — Silla royal burial mounds + Chomseongdae Observatory
- **7:15 PM:** **Wolji Pond** — night reflections (after sunset)

**Hotel:** L7 Haeundae by Lotte
**Transport:** 🚕 Taxi to Yonggungsa (15 min) + private driver Yonggungsa → Gyeongju → back to Busan (~8 hrs)
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Temples are atmospheric in rain. If heavy all day: Busan Spa Land (jjimjilbang in Centum City) + Museum 1 (immersive LED art).

---

## Day 9 — May 1 (Fri) | KTX to Seoul

- **9:00 AM:** **Blueline Park** — sky capsule (Mipo → Cheongsapo), taxi back
- **AM:** **Jagalchi Market**
- **AM:** **BIFF Square** — street food
- **Lunch:** **Gukje Market** — milmyeon (cold noodles)
- **PM:** 🚄 **KTX from Busan Station** → Seoul (2.5 hrs)
- **Dinner:** **Hongdae** — nightlife & dinner

**Hotel:** Shilla Stay Mapo Hongdae · Mapo, Seoul
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Markets and Blueline work in light rain. If heavy: skip Blueline, more time at covered markets, then KTX as planned.

---

## Day 10 — May 2 (Sat) | Hike OR Icheon — Pick One

- **Morning — Pick one:**
  - 🥾 **Option A: Bukhansan — Bukhansanseong** (hard) | 6.8 km · 3–4 hrs · +500m
  - 🥾 **Option B: Inwangsan Fortress Wall** (moderate) | 3.5 km · 1.5–2 hrs · +236m
  - 🏺 **Option C: Icheon Ceramic Festival**
- **Evening:** 🎮 **PC Bang** — Korean gaming cafe in Hongdae
- **Late Night:** 🏪 Convenience store ramyeon + banana milk at GS25
- **Night option:** 🛍️ **Dongdaemun Night Shopping** (open until 5 AM)

**Hotel:** Shilla Stay Mapo Hongdae
**🌧️ Rain plan:** Skip hikes (dangerous when wet). Instead: Icheon Ceramic Festival, Lotte World indoor theme park, Korean cooking class, or Leeum Samsung Museum of Art.

---

## Day 11 — May 3 (Sun) | DMZ + Palace + Farewell

- **Morning:** 🪖 **DMZ Tour** — half day
- **PM:** **Gyeongbokgung Palace**
- **PM:** **Bukchon Hanok Village**
- **Late PM:** **Gwangjang Market** — street food
- **Evening:** 🎭 **Optional: NANTA Show** — Myeongdong or Hongdae (~90 min)
- **Evening:** **Han River** — chimaek & farewell sunset

**Hotel:** Shilla Stay Mapo Hongdae
**🌧️ Rain plan:** DMZ runs rain or shine. Afternoon: National Museum of Korea (free), Dongdaemun shopping malls, or jjimjilbang (Dragon Hill Spa).

---

## Day 12 — May 4 (Mon) | Depart

- Sleep in, pack, last brunch near hotel
- **~14:00** Head to Incheon (AREX or taxi, ~1 hr)
- **17:50** Flight home
